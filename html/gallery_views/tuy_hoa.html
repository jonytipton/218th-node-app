Tuy Hoa Detachment

A detachment of the 218th MP Co was established in Tuy Hoa at the time of the 218th relocation to Nha Trang, some MPs arriving just as the Tet offensive began. Tuy Hoa was also the destination of frequent convoys from CRB.
